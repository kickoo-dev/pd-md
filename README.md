## How to contribute your PD.md

1. Fork this repo
2. Add your PD.md under `examples/<your-project-slug>/product_details.md`
3. Add a short intro in `examples/<your-project-slug>/README.md` describing:
   - What the product is
   - What Kickoo playable was generated from this PD.md
   - Any lessons learned from the distribution
4. Open a PR

We'll showcase your example on [kickoo.cn](https://kickoo.cn) and [pdmd.dev](https://pdmd.dev).
